INSTAGRAM SETUP GUIDE FOR THE UNJOURNAL
========================================

Note: Account creation requires phone app.

STEP 1: CREATE ACCOUNT (on phone)
---------------------------------
1. Download Instagram app
2. Sign up with contact@unjournal.org
3. Username options (check availability):
   - unjournal (ideal)
   - theunjournal
   - unjournal_org
4. Full name: "The Unjournal"

STEP 2: CONVERT TO BUSINESS ACCOUNT
-----------------------------------
1. Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account
2. Select "Business" (not Creator)
3. Category: "Science, Technology & Engineering" or "Education"
4. Connect to a Facebook Page (create one if needed — required for API/dlvr.it)

STEP 3: PROFILE SETUP
---------------------
Profile photo: Unjournal logo (same as other platforms)

Bio (150 chars max):
  Open peer evaluation of impactful research
  Economics • Policy • Global priorities
  New video ↓

Website: https://unjournal.org
Contact: contact@unjournal.org

STEP 4: CONNECT TO DLVR.IT
--------------------------
1. Log into dlvr.it
2. Destinations → Add New → Instagram
3. Authorize via Facebook
4. Add to existing route or create new one for visual content

Note: Instagram requires image/video with every post. dlvr.it can auto-generate
thumbnails from YouTube links, or you can attach custom images.


CONTENT STRATEGY
================

1. YouTube Video Clips (Reels)
   - Repost YouTube videos as Reels (under 90 seconds)
   - Caption: Brief description + "Full video: link in bio"

2. Quote Cards
   - Compelling quotes from evaluations
   - Simple design: quote on solid background with branding

3. Evaluation Announcements
   - Image: Paper title + key rating
   - Caption: Findings, significance, link to evaluation

4. Infographics
   - Mermaid diagrams as images
   - 6-step process as carousel
   - "How We Rate Research" explainer

5. Behind-the-Scenes
   - Team updates, events, milestones


FIRST 5 POSTS (suggested)
=========================

1. Introduction post
   Image: Unjournal logo or branded graphic
   Caption: "We're The Unjournal — providing open, rigorous evaluation of
   research that matters. No gatekeeping, no publication bias. Just transparent
   peer review with quantitative ratings. More at link in bio."

2. The Unjournal Process video (Reel)
   Upload the 2-minute YouTube video directly
   Caption: "How does open peer evaluation work? Our 6-step process in
   2 minutes. Full video on YouTube — link in bio."

3. Evaluator Prizes announcement
   Image: Graphic with prize winner names
   Caption: Brief announcement + link

4. Quote card from standout evaluation
   Compelling finding from a top evaluation
   Caption: Context + link to full evaluation

5. Carousel: What We Evaluate
   4-5 slides: global health, animal welfare, climate economics, AI policy, etc.
   Caption: "Research that informs decisions on the world's most pressing problems."


LIMITATIONS TO KNOW
===================
- Links aren't clickable in captions (only in bio)
- Every post needs an image or video (no text-only)
- Reels max 90 seconds
- Stories disappear after 24 hours
- "Link in bio" is standard workaround for sharing URLs
