Field Specialists & Research Affiliates

Over 50 researchers across multiple domains help us identify, prioritize, and evaluate impactful research. Our field specialists provide expertise in:

Development Economics & Global Health

Researchers focusing on poverty alleviation, health interventions, education, and development policy in low- and middle-income countries.

Environmental Economics & Climate

Specialists in climate policy, conservation, environmental regulation, and the economics of sustainability.

Behavioral Science & Psychology

Experts in decision-making, social psychology, other-regarding preferences, and behavioral interventions.

AI Governance & Technology Policy

Researchers studying the social impact of AI, technology regulation, and emerging technology governance.

Animal Welfare

Specialists in animal welfare economics, attitudes research, and market dynamics affecting animal wellbeing.

Catastrophic & Existential Risk

Researchers working on long-term risks, forecasting, and interventions for civilizational resilience.

Meta-Science & Research Methods

Experts in research methodology, replication, statistical methods, and the science of science.

For a complete list of our field specialists and research affiliates, see our full team documentation.
