Why The Unjournal?

Traditional academic publishing is slow, expensive, and often misaligned with real-world impact. The Unjournal provides an alternative: rigorous, transparent evaluation that prioritizes global priorities over prestige metrics.

💰

Reduced Publication Costs

No formatting hoops. No resubmission cycles. Submit your preprint or working paper as-is. We evaluate the research, not the formatting.

🔍

Transparent Assessment

Evaluations are public, creating valuable metrics and feedback for authors, researchers, and decision-makers. No more black-box peer review.

🌎

Global Impact Focus

Evaluators assess reliability, robustness, and usefulness—not just novelty. We connect rigorous research with organizations solving pressing global challenges.

🔬

Open & Robust Science

Supporting replicability and methodological rigor. Studies can be independently verified. Methods and limitations are clearly communicated.

📄

Living Research

Research can exist as interactive, web-based materials—not locked PDFs. Projects evolve through ongoing feedback, new data, and methodological refinements.

🤝

Non-Exclusive

Having your work evaluated by The Unjournal doesn't limit your options. You can still submit to any traditional journal, before, during, or after our process.

What You Get

For researchers: Expert feedback from paid evaluators, quantitative ratings across multiple dimensions, public credibility signals, and visibility in open science communities—all without giving up your publication rights.

Detailed Evaluation Reports

Each evaluation includes substantive written feedback plus quantitative ratings on:

  • Overall quality and credibility of the research
  • Methods—rigor, appropriateness, and limitations
  • Real-world relevance—usefulness for policy and practice
  • Communication—clarity and accessibility
  • Confidence bounds—evaluators express uncertainty in their ratings

Public Author Responses

Authors can respond to evaluations before or after publication. Responses receive their own DOI and are displayed alongside the evaluations, creating a transparent scholarly dialogue.

Quantitative Ratings

Unlike binary accept/reject decisions, our ratings provide nuanced credibility signals. Funders and policymakers can quickly assess research quality across standardized dimensions.
