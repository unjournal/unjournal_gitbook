What's This Tournament About?

Forecasting tournaments bring together diverse predictors — researchers, analysts, and curious thinkers — to estimate the probability of future events. This will be Metaculus' first tournament focused entirely on animal welfare.

Questions will cover topics like:

🌱
Plant-based market share
Will plant-based meat alternatives reach 10% of US meat sales by 2030?
🐔
Corporate commitments
What percentage of companies will fulfill their cage-free egg pledges?
🥚
Cultivated meat regulation
Will cultivated meat be approved for sale in the EU by 2027?
📈
Consumption trends
Will per-capita meat consumption decline in any G7 country by 2028?

These are illustrative examples. Final tournament questions are being finalized with Metaculus.

Why does this matter? Better predictions lead to better prioritization, which leads to better outcomes for animals. Forecasting tournaments help surface what the community actually believes — and where the biggest uncertainties lie.

How The Unjournal Is Involved

The Unjournal's Pivotal Questions project tackles critical uncertainties in effective altruism research. We're collaborating with this tournament to:

  • Help shape decision-relevant questions, drawing on our work on plant-based product substitution and cultured meat viability
  • Connect tournament forecasts with rigorous research evaluation
  • Integrate insights into our Metaculus community forecasts

Our goal: synthesize forecasting data with academic evidence to create actionable "decision packages" for funders and advocates.
