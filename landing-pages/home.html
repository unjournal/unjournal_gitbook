Making rigorous research more impactful, and impactful research more rigorous.

The Unjournal organizes expert evaluation of quantitative research bearing on global priorities — in economics, policy, social science, and related fields. We publish open, citable evaluations and numerical ratings. We work outside the traditional journal submission process, reducing delays and conflicts of interest.

We are not a journal — we don’t “accept” or “reject” papers. We evaluate research that already exists or is in progress, and publish those evaluations openly.

How It Works

1

We identify research

Our field specialists and management committee identify high-impact quantitative research in our priority areas. Authors can also submit or recommend their own work.

2

We commission evaluations

We invite 2–3 expert evaluators per paper, who provide structured written assessments and numerical ratings with confidence intervals across 7 criteria.

3

We publish openly

Evaluations are published on PubPub (unjournal.pubpub.org) with DOIs, alongside the original research. Authors receive feedback and can respond publicly.

For Researchers

Submit or Recommend Research

The Unjournal offers a fast, low-friction alternative to the traditional submission process.

  • Free to submit — no fees, no formatting requirements
  • Receive substantive expert feedback to strengthen your work
  • Evaluations are citable published outputs with DOIs
  • No journal rejection — evaluation is constructive, not gatekeeping
  • Evaluators rated against authors’ responses for accountability
  • Cash prizes for top-evaluated work (2024–25 program active)

For Evaluators

Become an Evaluator

We compensate evaluators for their time and expertise. Your evaluation is not invisible service — it is a published, citable scholarly output.

  • We pay $250–$400 per evaluation (plus incentive bonuses)
  • Work is flexible and fully remote
  • Your evaluation receives a DOI and is published under your name (or anonymously — your choice)
  • Join 200+ researchers who have already signed up
  • Annual prizes for exceptional evaluations ($6,500 awarded in 2024–25)

Complete a short form to be added to our database. We contact you when research matches your expertise.
