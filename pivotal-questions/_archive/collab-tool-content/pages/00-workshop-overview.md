# Wellbeing Measures: Research & Policy Discussion

An Unjournal Online Workshop on WELLBY Measurement, Scale-Use Heterogeneity, and DALY-WELLBY Interconvertibility.

---

## About This Workshop

Following The Unjournal's evaluation of Benjamin et al.'s paper on "Adjusting for Scale-Use Heterogeneity in Self-Reported Well-Being", this workshop brings together researchers, evaluators, and practitioners to discuss:

- **WELLBY reliability** for cross-intervention comparison
- **DALY-WELLBY conversion** methods for cost-effectiveness analysis
- **Practical implications** for funders and researchers

## Pivotal Questions

This workshop addresses **11 Pivotal Questions** across two categories:

### WELLBY Reliability (7 questions)
- WELL_01: How reliable is the WELLBY relative to other wellbeing measures?
- WELL_02-09: Measurement approaches, cardinality, neutral points

### DALY-WELLBY Conversion (4 questions)
- DALY_01: Best numerical conversion between WELLBYs and DALYs
- DALY_02-05: Mapping methods for practical funding decisions
