# Beliefs Elicitation

**Duration:** 15 minutes

---

## Overview

A guided session where participants state their priors on operationalized pivotal questions. This creates a snapshot of expert beliefs that can be compared before/after the workshop and tracked over time.

---

## How It Works

1. **Complete the beliefs form** (link below)
2. **State your probability estimates** for key questions
3. **Provide reasoning** for your estimates
4. **Include uncertainty bounds** where applicable

---

**[Complete the Beliefs Elicitation Form](https://uj-wellbeing-workshop.netlify.app/beliefs.html)**

## Key Questions

### PQ1a: WELLBY Best Probability
*What is the probability that the linear WELLBY is the best or near-best measure for comparing interventions?*

### PQ2a: DALY Conversion Factor
*What is your best estimate for 1 DALY = X WELLBYs?*
- Point estimate
- 80% confidence interval (lower, upper)

### PQ3a-c: Predictions
- Research uptake by 2030
- Expert consensus by 2030
- Impact of calibration methods

---

## Discussion Prompts

1. **State your probability** that linear WELLBY is best/near-best measure

2. **State your DALY→WELLBY conversion factor estimate** with uncertainty

3. **Predict research adoption and expert consensus** by 2030
