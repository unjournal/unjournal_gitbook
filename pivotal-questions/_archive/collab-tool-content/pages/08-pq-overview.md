# Pivotal Questions

Key questions driving workshop discussion. These are high-value-of-information research questions where expert input can significantly update our understanding.

---

## What are Pivotal Questions?

Pivotal Questions (PQs) are research questions where:
1. The answer has significant practical implications
2. Expert disagreement exists
3. Resolution would update funding/policy decisions
4. Workshop discussion can generate meaningful signal

---

## Categories

### WELLBY Reliability (7 questions)

Questions about whether the linear WELLBY is a reliable measure for comparing interventions across different domains.

**Key themes:**
- Cardinality assumptions
- Neutral point calibration
- Dimensionality vs simplicity trade-offs
- Life satisfaction vs experiential measures

[→ Go to WELLBY Reliability page](#)

### DALY-WELLBY Conversion (4 questions)

Questions about how to convert between health metrics (DALYs/QALYs) and wellbeing metrics (WELLBYs) for cost-effectiveness analysis.

**Key themes:**
- Numerical conversion factors
- SD-equivalence approaches
- Practical mapping for funders
