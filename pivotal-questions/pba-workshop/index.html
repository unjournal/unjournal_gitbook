Plant-Based Substitution Workshop

How do plant-based products substitute for animal products? What does this mean for animal welfare funding decisions?

Target: May 2026 · Fully online · ~2.5 hours of live sessions + asynchronous participation
  • Scheduling
  • About
  • Pivotal Questions

This workshop examines the Plant-Based Substitution Pivotal Question: how much do plant-based alternatives actually displace animal product consumption? Understanding this is crucial for animal welfare funders deciding between PBA investments and other interventions.
