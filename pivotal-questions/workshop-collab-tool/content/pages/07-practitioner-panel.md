## Key Questions for Practitioners

### For Funders
- How should you handle WELLBY-DALY comparisons in grant decisions?
- What level of uncertainty should you communicate to donors?
- When is "good enough" measurement actually good enough?

### For Researchers
- What data should you collect to enable better comparisons?
- How should you report uncertainty in wellbeing measures?
- What calibration methods are feasible in field settings?

### For Evaluators
- How should you weight methodological concerns vs practical utility?
- What standards should exist for wellbeing measurement in CEA?
