# PQ: DALY-WELLBY Conversion

Questions about converting between health metrics (DALYs/QALYs) and wellbeing metrics (WELLBYs).

---

## Why This Matters

Funders comparing interventions across domains need a conversion:
- **GiveWell** uses DALYs for physical health interventions
- **Mental health** researchers often report WELLBYs
- **Without conversion**, cross-domain comparison is impossible

Current rough estimates suggest **1 DALY ≈ 2-15 WELLBYs** depending on method.

---

## Questions in This Category

- Key consideration: Sensitivity of funding decisions to this number
