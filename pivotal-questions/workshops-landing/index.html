Pivotal Questions Workshops

Expert workshops to synthesize evidence and elicit beliefs on high-value research questions

The Pivotal Questions Initiative identifies research questions where resolving uncertainty could substantially improve funding decisions in global health, animal welfare, and other cause areas.

These workshops bring together researchers, funders, and practitioners to review evidence, discuss methodological challenges, and elicit expert beliefs on key uncertainties.
