Metaculus Forecasts

Formal forecasting questions on Metaculus for crowd predictions. These questions were designed based on the workshop's Pivotal Questions to enable quantified forecasting.

DALY_01

What is the best numerical conversion factor between WELLBYs and DALYs?

If a charity prevents 1 DALY (Disability-Adjusted Life Year), approximately how many WELLBYs does this represent? Current estimates range from 2-15.

View on Metaculus →
WELL_04

Is a single WELLBY measure better than measuring dimensions separately?

For comparing interventions affecting both mental and physical health, is it better to use a single WELLBY measure or separate dimensional measures that are then combined?

View on Metaculus →
WELL_08

Should wellbeing CEA rely on life satisfaction or experience measures?

For cost-effectiveness analysis, should the primary metric be based on life satisfaction (evaluative) or momentary experience/affect measures (experiential)?

View on Metaculus →
WELL_01a

How much more cost-effective would optimal measurement be vs linear WELLBY?

Consider allocating $100,000 optimally using the best feasible measure vs linear WELLBY. What ratio of costs achieves equivalent welfare outcomes?
