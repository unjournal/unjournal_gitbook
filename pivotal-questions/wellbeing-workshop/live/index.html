🔬 The Focal Case: StrongMinds vs AMF

How should funders compare mental health interventions (StrongMinds, Friendship Bench) measured in WELLBYs against bednet distribution (AMF) measured in DALYs? HLI estimates ~47 WELLBYs per $1,000; GiveWell puts StrongMinds at 5–80% as cost-effective as AMF—a 16× range driven by methodological choices. This comparison motivates the workshop.
