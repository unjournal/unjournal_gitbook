Overview

The final segment brings together the workshop's insights into practical recommendations. Matt Lerner (Founders Pledge) and Peter Hickman (Coefficient Giving) will deliver brief presentations (~10 min each) on practical implications and reactions to workshop content, followed by open Q&A. A private follow-up discussion among heavily involved participants will follow.
