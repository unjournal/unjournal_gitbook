Meeting Format: Zoom Meeting with Breakout Rooms

Standard Zoom Meeting

Standard meeting format allows all participants to unmute and share screens. Ideal for our interactive workshop with ~20 participants.

  • Capacity: 100 participants (your Pro plan limit — sufficient for this workshop)
  • Duration: Up to 30 hours (plenty for our 5-hour workshop)
  • Interactivity: High — anyone can speak/share
  • Breakout rooms: Enabled with broadcast, voice, and flexible room management
  • Recording: Cloud + local both available
  • Captions: Automated captions enabled in multiple languages
Alternative formats for future events

Zoom Webinar

Webinar format separates "panelists" (can speak) from "attendees" (view-only by default). Better for large audiences.

  • Capacity: 500-10,000+ depending on license
  • Interactivity: Lower — attendees must be promoted to speak
  • Built-in Q&A feature for audience questions
  • Built-in registration page

Best for: Public-facing events with large audiences

Hybrid (Meeting + Livestream)

Run an interactive meeting while simultaneously streaming to YouTube/LinkedIn for broader audience.

  • Core participants in Zoom meeting, can interact
  • Public audience watches livestream, submits questions via chat
  • Higher complexity — requires managing two platforms

Best for: Events wanting both intimate discussion AND public broadcast

Zoom AI Companion Features

AI Companion is Zoom's built-in AI assistant. It can generate meeting summaries, answer questions about discussed content, and create action items — all processing happens on Zoom's infrastructure.

Recommended AI Settings

  • Meeting Summary: Enable — generates post-meeting summary automatically
  • Smart Recording: Enable — creates chapters, highlights, and next steps
  • In-Meeting Questions: Enable — participants can ask "what was said about X?"
  • Live Transcript: Enable — real-time captions + searchable transcript
How to enable AI Companion
  1. Go to zoom.us/profile/setting
  2. Navigate to "AI Companion" section
  3. Enable:
    • "Meeting summary with AI Companion"
    • "AI Companion questions in meetings"
    • "Smart recording with AI Companion"
  4. Set default for all meetings, or enable per-meeting

Note: Participants will see a notification that AI features are active.

Privacy considerations

When AI Companion is enabled:

  • Participants see a small AI icon and notification
  • Meeting content is processed by Zoom's AI (not stored for training)
  • Summaries can be shared with all participants or just the host
  • Participants can opt out of having their audio processed

For our workshop: Most participants have consented to recording, so AI processing should be acceptable. Mention it in the opening remarks.

Recording Setup

Recording consent: Matt Lerner and Peter Hickman initially preferred internal-only recording but have since indicated flexibility. Confirm final approach before the workshop.

Cloud Recording (Recommended)

  • Stored in Zoom cloud — easy to share and download
  • Automatic transcription included
  • Can generate AI summary and chapters
  • 720p/1080p quality depending on settings
  • Separate audio tracks for each participant (useful for editing)

Local Recording

  • Saved to host's computer
  • Higher quality possible (up to 1080p)
  • No cloud storage usage
  • Must manually upload to share
Post-processing options

For queryable transcript:

  • Download Zoom's auto-generated VTT transcript
  • Upload to a service like Typeset or Descript for AI-queryable version
  • Or embed in a custom interface with search functionality

For public sharing:

  • YouTube (unlisted or public)
  • Vimeo (more control over embedding)
  • Direct link from Zoom cloud

Meeting Link & Invitation Options

Single Persistent Link (Recommended)

One meeting link for the entire workshop. Participants can drop in/out for specific segments.

  • Simpler — one link to share and remember
  • Waiting room can control entry between segments
  • Continuous recording captures everything

Separate Links per Segment

Different meeting links for each segment (Stakeholder, Paper, Evaluator, etc.)

  • Easier to track attendance per segment
  • Clean recording boundaries
  • More complex — multiple links to manage
Registration options

No registration (simple): Just share the meeting link directly.

Zoom registration: Participants register via Zoom, receive unique link. Useful for tracking attendance.

External registration: Use our Netlify form for interest, then send Zoom link separately.

Recommendation: Given we have a confirmed participant list, skip formal registration. Send the link directly to confirmed participants via email.
