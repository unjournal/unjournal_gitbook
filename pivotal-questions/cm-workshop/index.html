Cultivated Meat: Cost Trajectories & Welfare Implications

An Unjournal Workshop on Techno-Economic Analyses, Commercial Viability, and Animal Welfare ROI

Following our evaluation of Rethink Priorities' cultured meat forecasting work and ongoing TEA evaluations, this workshop explores what the evidence tells us about cultivated meat's cost trajectory and its implications for animal welfare funding decisions.
